Laser Beam

Laser Beam is a data visualization project showing third party trackers on popular websites.

The center nodes represent the first party websites and the surronding nodes represent the third party trackers. The network graph is interactive: users can drag and drop within the contained box; while hovering on the center node, the third-party tracker names will appear; while clicking on the center node, the website and its trackers will light up - just like a laser beam!

Try the filter to select or deselect any companies - the graph will update instantly! The Bar-chart is also interactive! Try it out!
